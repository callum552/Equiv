Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 February 2026

Overview

Equiv is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Equiv does not collect any user data of any kind. This includes:

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

On-Device Processing

All unit conversions are performed entirely on your device. Your conversion history and favourites are stored locally using Apple's on-device frameworks and are never transmitted off your device.

Currency Exchange Rates

The currency conversion feature fetches live exchange rates from a third-party API (Open Exchange Rates). This network request does not include any personal or identifying information. No account or API key tied to your identity is used. The rates are cached locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Equiv does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Equiv does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
